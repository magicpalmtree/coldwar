Cold War

Coldwar is a simulation platform. You can explore it's built in scenes, or use it as a starting point for writing your own.

The platform builds on concepts presented at JSConf.US 2015, Web Directions Code (video), TX.js (video), JSConf.Asia 2014 (video) and Talk.js Singapore November 2015 video).

Source is at github.com/simonswain/coldwar

TAB toggles scene options
ENTER restarts scene
\ toggles diagnostics
? toggles help
Mousewheel and Drag to zoom and pan
+/- to zoom
0 to reset zoom
ESC to get back to Index',
